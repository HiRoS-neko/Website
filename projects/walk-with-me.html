Walk With Me

During fourth year, in a group of thirteen classmates I collaborated and designed a game over the course of 8 months.
2 months dedicated to preproduction and planning, and 6 months dedicated to production. Unfortunately due to the events of COVID-19, production had to be halted before completion of the game.
We made Walk With Me as Knick Knack Game Shack.


About

Walk With Me is a co-operative, 3D, isometric, adventure game where players take on the roles of The Reaper and The Lost Soul as they travel through The Netherworld. The players must explore, solve puzzles, and fend off enemies as they journey towards the afterlife.


Role

As lead programmer, I worked on player input, game mechanics, AI systems, event driven animation, and UI/UX.
